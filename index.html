Your Coach

Angharad is a nutritionist and health & lifestyle coach based in Hokkaido, Japan.

Supporting and guiding both women and men to implement positive health & lifestyle changes into their lives, Angharad graduated as a qualified nutritionist with a BSc (Hons) Food & Nutrition in 2009, and has since gone on to complete her accreditation as a Health & Life Coach after 15 years as a nutritionist. She has been coaching clients on their health journeys since 2021.

Qualifications:

  • Food & Nutrition, Manchester Metropolitan University, BSc (Hons)
  • Health and Life Coach, CCE-Accredited by International Coaching Federation
  • 200 Hour Vinyasa Flow, Nadi Wellness
  • 90 Hour Aerial Yoga, Aerial Yoga Academy
